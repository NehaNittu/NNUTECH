Quality & Testing

Rigorous multi-stage quality control on every PCB we deliver

Process

Our 6-Stage Quality Process

From design review to final delivery — every stage verified before shipping

01

Design Review (DFM / DFA)

Manufacturability and assembly checks before production begins — identifying and resolving potential issues early to prevent costly rework later.

02

Visual Inspection

Manual visual inspection of solder joints, component placements, and board condition against IPC workmanship standards.

03

AOI Inspection

Automated Optical Inspection detects solder defects, missing components, polarity errors, and placement deviations with high accuracy and full board coverage.

04

Electrical Testing

Continuity and isolation testing verifies correct connections and the absence of shorts or open circuits across the entire board net-list.

05

Functional Testing

Full functional validation under real operating conditions confirms every board performs to specification before shipment.

06

Final Inspection & Delivery

Complete final review with quality documentation, careful packaging, and delivery — every board signed off before it leaves our hands.
